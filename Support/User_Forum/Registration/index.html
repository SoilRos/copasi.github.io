User Forum Registration

Posting to the COPASI User Forum is limited to registered members to prevent spamming. To post and reply via the web or email, you will need to have a Google account . If you use any non-GMail addresses, make sure to associate those with your Google account. This will allow you to send e-mail replies to the group and, importantly, have the group automatically recognize you as the same user as from your Google account. You should also be able to set up a non-GMail address as your primary e-mail, when you set up a Google account.
