2016 Conference on Computational Modelling with COPASI

The Conference takes place on May 12th and 13th, 2016, in the Manchster Institute of Biotechnology. All sessions take place in the MIB Auditorium, all breaks, meals, and poster sessions take place in the MIB Atrium (outside the Auditorium).

Thursday, 12th May
9:15–9:30 Welcome Address
9:30–10:15 Invited Talk: Ines Heiland, The Arctic University of Norway, Tromsø: Tissue specific models of Trp- and NAD-metabolism — insights into metabolic crosstalk"
10:15–11:05 Session 1 (Chair: Norman Paton)
  • Jens Hahn: From Pieces to the Whole — Concepts for Modular Modelling of Yeast.
  • Vijayalakshmi Chelliah: BioModels: a public model sharing resource for computational models of biological/biomedical systems.
  • Chuan Fu Yap: Towards automated construction of kinetic metabolic models with GRaPe.
