Travel

Flights

There are direct flights to Manchester Airport from over 200 destinations.

From Manchester Airport there are frequent trains to Manchester Piccadilly train station (duration between 12 and 18 minutes, price £4.00-£7.00). You can also take a taxi to the city centre (cost around £25)

In addition, Liverpool John Lennon Airport is 1 hour away from Manchester city centre by direct bus. Liverpool Airport is served by several budget airlines with direct connections to a number of locations in Europe.

Trains

Manchester is easily accessible from other locations in the UK. Direct trains to London from Manchester Piccadilly have a duration of about 2 hours. Train routes and schedules available from National Rail Equiries

The conference venue is within walking distance from Manchester Piccadilly and Oxford Road trains stations (see map below, in green).

Accomodation

The free registration does not include accomodation. Each delegate has to make their own accommodation arrangements. Hotels very near the location of the conference are:

  • Pendulum Hotel Manchester (just behind the conference venue).
  • Ibis Princess Street (2 minute walk to conference venue).
